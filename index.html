Dr. Yue Qiu

Postdoc Researcher

Max Planck Institute for Dynamics of Complex Technical Systems

I am a postdoc researcher of Computational Methods in Systems and Control Theory Group headed by Prof. Dr. Peter Benner at Max Planck Institute for Dynamics of Complex Technical Systems, Magdeburg, Germany.

My research interests focus on

  • Preconditioning Technique
  • PDE-Constrained Optimization
  • Structured Matrix Computations
  • Numerical Linear Algebra for Uncertainty Quantification
  • Structure-Preserving Model Order Reduction
  • Wind Farm Control
  • Multidisciplinary research involving mathematics and systems & control
To summarize, my interest are on efficient numerical methods for control and optimization of large-scale dynamical systems.
