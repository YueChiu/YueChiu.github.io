Welcome to my personal website

I am a postdoc researcher in the Computational Methods in Systems and Control Theory group, headed by Prof. dr. Peter Benner at Max Planck Institute for Dynamics of Complex Technical Systems, Magdeburg, Germany.

Prior to joining MPI, I did my PhD in applied mathematics in the Numerical Analysis group at Delft University of Technology, Delft, the Netherlands. I conduct my PhD research on preconditioning saddle-point systems with applications to flow control problems under the supervision of Prof. dr. Martin van Gijzen. Part of my PhD work is also performed at Delft Center for Systems and Control, under the supervision of Prof. dr. Jan-Willem van Wingerden.

My research interest/focuses are:

  • numerical analysis
  • numerical linear algebra
  • uncertainty quantification
  • PDE-constrained optimization
  • energy networks
